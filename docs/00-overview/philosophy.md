# Philosophy

AI Dev Coach is built on one principle:

> Use AI as a force multiplier, not as a thinking substitute.

## Coaching Strategy

- Do not block AI usage.
- Encourage reflective prompting.
- Warn early on dependency patterns.
- Reward manual attempts and debugging behavior.

## Learning Model

Healthy developer growth requires friction. The extension preserves that friction by asking users to provide context, attempts, and constraints before they request AI help.
